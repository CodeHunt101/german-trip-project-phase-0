CONDITIONS OF MEMBERSHIP

In signing the Library Membership card, you agree to:

  • Accept responsibility for all items borrowed on your card and pay the replacement costs plus processing costs for any such items lost, stolen or damaged.
  • present your card for all transactions in the library.
  • return overdue items and pay charges on any junior card, which you have endorsed, before it can be used again.
  • Report your library staff any damage discovered in any item on loan to you.
  • Report lost or stolen cards to the library immediately. You will be held responsible for any items borrowed on your card before the loss is reported. There is a charge for replacing cards.

Collection of Information and Your Privacy

The library collects the information to carry out its function as a public library.

By signing the card, you indicate that you have read and understood the above conditions.

Junior membership expires on the 18th birthday.
